The Bias—

Racial biases and Stereotyping

Though it has been over 60 years since the Supreme Court declared that segregated schools are unconstitutional, the biases and stereotypes surrounding race have been perpetuated and expanded upon. Due to economics, the comfort of being part of a community, and other factors, many schools have resegregated.

Falter from Graduation—

Rate per 100,000 of Placement of Juveniles in Residential Facilities

The white bars represent men, the black bars represent women

For every 100,000 students, 733 black males are placed in Juvenile Residential Facilities in comparison to just 153 white males or 50 Asian males. All over the United States, there are 1,400 high schools that have graduation rates of 40% or lower. Most of these schools are made up of predominantly minority students. Though dropout rates have evened out amongst different races, the current outlier is Asian American Students, who have a dropout rate of 1.8% in comparison to 4.7% average.
